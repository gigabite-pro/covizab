Features

Booking
Choose a vaccination according to your age group and then select an available time slot. If no time slot is available you can choose to get notified when one opens up.
QR Code
You'll get a QR Code once you have paid for a dose. Just show the QR Code and nothing else on vaccination day to get the dose.
Paperless
Your digitally signed vaccination certificate will be mailed to you which you can show anywhere to prove you're vaccinated.
Post Vaccination Consultation
Get up to 5 free sessions with our specialized doctors to help you with any problems you face after taking your vaccine.
Vaccination Notification
If you've have taken the first dose through our service, after the waiting period we'll notify you about the discounted second dose.
