About

The main takeaway from the project was realizing that are a lot of factors to consider like authenticity, notifications, accessibility when building a vaccine delivering platform.
Why?
All the unpleasant things going on in the country prompted us to research about the covid vaccines. We wanted to know how the vaccination services worked, how they scheduled appointments and what were the benefits of getting a certificate. All this gave us a sense of purpose and we decided to make our own vaccination service website.

The project has made us realise how benefitiary these vaccine services really are.
How?
With Covizab you can-
- Schedule appointments on you devices.
- Get a QR Code of the certificate online.
- Get health advice after getting vaccine.

Covizab provides users with a valid certificate after getting vaccinated for international travelling with the help of a QR Code which can be scanned anywhere. It also provides you with a discounted second dose.
What?
Covizab is a a country wide vaccination service. Our website contains of 5 modules, an about section, a pricing section, features of our website, feedback form and FAQs.

The objective of our service is to help in the immunization of India from Covid-19 by providing safe vaccines at a reasonable cost to everyone.

Our website is user-friendly and can be accessed by anyone. We also aim to make this service free in the future.

For Adults
₹ 900/dose
This dose is meant for people aged above 18. The dosage will only be administered if the adult hasn't contracted COVID-19 in the past fourteen days. The mandated waiting period for the next dose is 6 weeks.

Features

Booking
Choose a vaccination according to your age group and then select an available time slot. If no time slot is available you can choose to get notified when one opens up.
QR Code
You'll get a QR Code once you have paid for a dose. Just show the QR Code and nothing else on vaccination day to get the dose.
Paperless
Your digitally signed vaccination certificate will be mailed to you which you can show anywhere to prove you're vaccinated.
Post Vaccination Consultation
Get up to 5 free sessions with our specialized doctors to help you with any problems you face after taking your vaccine.
Vaccination Notification
If you've have taken the first dose through our service, after the waiting period we'll notify you about the discounted second dose.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is it mandatory to take the vaccine?

Vaccination is currently not mandatory. It is however always better to get vaccinated and limit the spread of the Covid-19 virus.

Q. Can a person presently having COVID-19 infection be vaccinated?

No, the person will be spreading the virus and is not fit to receive the vaccination. Everyone with a confirmed case has to wait 3 months before getting vaccinated.

Q. Who all are eligible to get vaccinated currently in India?

Anyone who is 18 years and older is eligible to get the vaccine against Covid-19. Though availability may vary by state.

Q. What not to do after vaccination?

Avoid strenous activites or other things which induce stress for about 2-3 days post vaccination. Also, avoid any kind of drug including alcohol and tobacco.
